INTRODUCTION

Welcome to the Proactive Project Management Web App

The Proactive Project Management Web App is a web-based project management application that allows you to manage your projects efficiently. With our app, you can:

  • Track your project's progress in real-time
  • Collaborate with your team members
  • Manage resources efficiently
  • Generate detailed reports and analytics

Sign up today to take advantage of our comprehensive suite of tools and take your project management to the next level.

FEATURES

The Proactive Project Management Web App provides a comprehensive suite of tools to help you manage your projects efficiently:

  • Task management
  • Team collaboration
  • Project progress tracking
  • Resource management
  • Customizable project templates
  • Automated reminders and notifications

ABOUT

Our Story

Proactive Project Management Web App was born out of a personal need for a cheaper project management tool. I wasn't about to pay for any tool so I decided to make mine.

Over the past few weeks, I have worked hard, strived, cried and almost gave up over this project but I am proud to say that I have worked hard to create a tool that would help us manage our projects efficiently.

This project is also a part of my ALX project portfolio, showcasing my skills thus far from the ALX Software engineering course which lasts for over a year.
